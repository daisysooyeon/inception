Story

The story line of inception

Steal your thoughts VS A future society where you can connect with other people's dreams with a machine called "Beware of the Mind." Dom Cobb is a thief who steals thoughts with his best ability. By accident, he became an international wanted man, and now he has a chance to recover everything thanks to the inter-business war. But the task is not to steal information from the head, but to put it in the head! He organizes a strong team to succeed in this operation called the 'Inception.' It is a nearly impossible game, but you must win!
